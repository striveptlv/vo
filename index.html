Vestibular Rehabilitation

This vestibular rehabilitation tool is designed to support recovery from dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders through guided, evidence-based exercises. It allows clinicians and patients to safely practice gaze stabilization, balance training, and motion tolerance activities in a structured, user-friendly format. With adjustable difficulty levels and real-world simulations, the tool helps bridge the gap between clinic and daily life—promoting confidence, independence, and functional recovery.

Choose one of the practice tool and exercise focus.
